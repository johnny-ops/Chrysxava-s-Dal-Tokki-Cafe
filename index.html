Our Story

Inspired by the legendary Moon Rabbit Tokki

Once upon a time, in a peaceful forest, there lived a rabbit named Tokki. One night, the Jade Emperor wanted to find a creature with a big heart. Tokki's pure kindness earned her a place on the moon, where she helps others by making rice cakes.

Now, whenever people look at the moon, they see Tokki and remember her kindness, teaching everyone that even the smallest heart can shine the brightest. This spirit of warmth and generosity inspires everything we do at Dal Tokki Cafe.
